    About Maharshi Tyagi Higher Secondary School

    Having a hard working and a dedicated Principal Mr. R.P. Maurya has not only helped the school reaching new heights but also improved the status of the school in the region. Under the Enthusiastic approach, our respected principal Mr. R.P. Maurya developed a culture for the personality development of students through several competition and activities in the school.

    The School is an attempt to provide a definite shape to the long awaited dream to cater quality education to the children of the locality and beyond.

    Our Value system is griding light enabling us to bring about a positive change even if it's a small for one beneficiary.
